# Platform Roadmap: 5-Year Evolution of AI Office

AI Office is built for 5-10 year evolution without architectural redesign. This roadmap outlines the planned development from initial launch through mature enterprise platform.

---

## Roadmap Philosophy

**Principles**:
1. **Foundation First**: Governance, then knowledge, then execution, then experience
2. **Evolutionary**: Add features and capabilities, don't redesign
3. **Learning**: Each phase informs the next
4. **User-Driven**: Features address real user needs
5. **Sustainable**: Architecture supports long-term growth

---

## Risk Management

### High-Risk Roadmap Items

| Item | Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------|-----------|
| Year 2 Multi-company support | Complexity | Pilot with one company first |
| Year 3 Advanced automation | User acceptance | Extensive testing, gradual rollout |
| Year 4 External integrations | Security | Sandbox, rate limiting, audit |
| Year 5 Decentralized features | Regulatory | Legal review, compliance first |

This roadmap is **not fixed**. Adjustments based on:
- User feedback (most important)
- Market changes
- Technology advances
- Regulatory requirements

**Quarterly reviews** assess:

- Are estimates accurate?
- Should priorities change?
- What did we learn?
