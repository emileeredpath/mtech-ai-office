## Monthly Routine Purpose

The monthly routine is when Sandy:

- Conducts comprehensive organizational assessment
- Reviews performance against strategic objectives
- Makes strategic adjustments
- Plans next month
- Creates performance reports
- Communicates with leadership

**Duration**: 8-10 hours across the month

**Timing**: Last week of month and first few days of next month

## Monthly Schedule

### First Meeting: Strategic Planning (Monthly, First Monday, 9:00-11:00 AM)

**Purpose**: Review strategy, assess alignment, make priority decisions

**Participants**: Managing Director, Sandy, project owners

**Agenda**:
1. Strategic progress review
2. Objective achievement status
3. Priority assessment
4. Resource needs
5. Risk assessment
6. New initiatives
7. Decision points

**Outcomes**:
- Strategic direction is confirmed or adjusted
- Priorities are set for month
- Resource decisions are made

### Second Meeting: Comprehensive Performance Review (Monthly, Last Thursday)

**Purpose**: Comprehensive review of all organizational metrics and performance

**Participants**: Sandy, project owners, key stakeholders

**Agenda**:
1. Project portfolio health
2. Quality and compliance status
3. Resource utilization
4. Schedule performance
5. Risk status
6. Team engagement
7. Process improvement status

**Outcomes**:
- Full performance picture is clear
- Trends are identified
- Issues are escalated

### Third Meeting: All-Hands Meeting (Monthly, Third Thursday, 3:00-4:00 PM)

**Purpose**: Share organizational progress, celebrate achievements, align team

**Participants**: All team members

**Agenda**:
1. Strategic progress
2. Project highlights
3. Team recognition
4. Organizational news
5. Q&A with leadership
6. Team building

**Outcomes**:
- Team is informed and aligned
- Achievements are celebrated
- Morale is maintained

## Monthly Analysis (6-8 hours)

Beyond meetings, Sandy conducts:

### 1. Performance Data Analysis (2 hours)

**Activities**:

1. **Gather Performance Data**
   - Collect all metrics for the month
   - Verify accuracy
   - Organize by category

2. **Analyze Performance**
   - Compare to targets
   - Analyze trends
   - Identify variances
   - Assess root causes

3. **Performance Categories**:
   - Strategic alignment (% of work aligned to objectives)
   - Project performance (on-time, quality, budget)
   - Quality metrics (standards compliance, rework rates)
   - Resource utilization
   - Schedule performance
   - Risk status
   - Team engagement/satisfaction

4. **Trend Analysis**
   - Month-to-month trends
   - Quarter-to-date trends
   - Year-to-date trends
   - Improvement/deterioration patterns

**Outcome**: Clear understanding of organizational performance

### 2. Strategic Assessment (1.5 hours)

**Activities**:

1. **Strategic Objective Progress**
   - Progress toward each stated objective
   - Pace vs. targets
   - Risks to objectives
   - Opportunities for acceleration

2. **Strategic Alignment**
   - What % of effort is aligned to strategy?
   - Are there diversions from strategy?
   - Should priorities be adjusted?
   - Are there capability gaps?

3. **Strategic Challenges**
   - What is impeding strategic progress?
   - What risks threaten strategy?
   - What decisions are needed?
   - What escalations are required?

4. **Strategic Outlook**
   - What is next month's focus?
   - What decisions are coming?
   - What preparation is needed?
   - What opportunities exist?

**Outcome**: Strategic alignment is clear; next month is planned

### 3. Risk and Issue Assessment (1 hour)

**Activities**:

1. **Risk Landscape**
   - How many active risks?
   - What is overall risk level?
   - Are risks increasing or decreasing?
   - What is the risk trajectory?

2. **Issue Resolution**
   - What issues were open at month start?
   - How many were resolved?
   - How many are still open?
   - How long are issues staying open?

3. **Crisis Assessment**
   - Were there any crises this month?
   - If so, what was learned?
   - What changes are needed to prevent recurrence?

4. **Escalation Status**
   - What escalations occurred?
   - Were they resolved?
   - What decisions are pending?
   - What action is needed?

**Outcome**: Risk profile is clear; escalations are tracked

### 4. Team Health Assessment (1 hour)

**Activities**:

1. **Engagement and Satisfaction**
   - Team satisfaction score
   - Engagement signals
   - Turnover or retention issues
   - Team dynamics

2. **Development and Growth**
   - Development opportunities provided
   - Skills being built
   - Career progression
   - Concerns or issues

3. **Workload and Sustainability**
   - Is pace sustainable?
   - Are people overloaded?
   - Are there burnout signals?
   - What adjustments are needed?

4. **Team Dynamics**
   - Is team functioning well?
   - Are there conflicts?
   - Is collaboration strong?
   - What improvements are needed?

**Outcome**: Team health is clear; support is planned where needed

### 5. Learning and Improvement (1 hour)

**Activities**:

1. **Learning Capture**
   - What did we learn this month?
   - What went particularly well?
   - What went poorly?
   - What surprised us?

2. **Process Improvements**
   - What process improvements have been made?
   - What improvements should be made?
   - What is the ROI of improvements?
   - What should be priorities?

3. **Knowledge Documentation**
   - What knowledge should be captured?
   - What processes are documented?
   - What gaps exist?
   - What updates are needed?

4. **Improvement Tracking**
   - What improvements were implemented?
   - Are they delivering expected value?
   - What adjustments are needed?
   - What new improvements should be started?

**Outcome**: Learning is captured; improvements are planned

### 6. Monthly Report Creation (2 hours)

**Activities**:

1. **Data Organization**
   - Organize performance data
   - Create charts and visualizations
   - Prepare supporting detail

2. **Narrative Development**
   - Write key findings
   - Explain trends and variances
   - Provide context and interpretation
   - Make recommendations

3. **Report Structure**
   - Executive summary
   - Strategic progress
   - Project portfolio
   - Performance metrics
   - Quality and compliance
   - Risk assessment
   - Team status
   - Recommendations and outlook

4. **Report Review**
   - Verify accuracy
   - Check completeness
   - Ensure clarity
   - Prepare for presentation

**Outcome**: Comprehensive monthly performance report

## Monthly Communication

**To Managing Director**: Monthly performance report with strategic assessment

**To Project Owners**: Monthly project portfolio status with individual project details

**To Team**: All-hands meeting with progress, recognition, and alignment

**To Stakeholders**: Status updates on their areas of responsibility

## End of Month Checklist

- [ ] All monthly meetings conducted
- [ ] Performance data collected and analyzed
- [ ] Strategic assessment completed
- [ ] Risk and issue status reviewed
- [ ] Team health assessed
- [ ] Learning captured and documented
- [ ] Monthly performance report completed
- [ ] Month-end communication sent
- [ ] Next month is planned
- [ ] Strategic priorities confirmed
- [ ] Resource plan for next month developed
- [ ] Any escalations from month are documented

## Beginning of Next Month Checklist

- [ ] Previous month's report is filed
- [ ] This month's priorities are confirmed
- [ ] Team is briefed on month ahead
- [ ] Strategic focus is clear
- [ ] Risk mitigation is planned
- [ ] Projects are scoped
- [ ] Resources are allocated
- [ ] Deadlines are scheduled
- [ ] Success measures are established

## Quarterly Deep Dives

In months that contain quarter-end, add:

**Quarterly Business Review (4 hours)**:
- Comprehensive quarter assessment
- Trend analysis across quarter
- Strategic progress evaluation
- Organizational capability assessment
- Recommendations for next quarter

## Annual Planning (December)

In December, annual planning occurs:

**Strategic Planning (8 hours)**:
- Year in review
- Achievement of annual objectives
- Organizational capability assessment
- Learning and improvements from year
- Strategy for next year
- Objectives for next year
- Resource planning for next year
- Risk assessment for next year

## Monthly Reflection

Each month, Sandy reflects:

- **Strategic Progress**: Are we making progress toward objectives?
- **Team Health**: Is team engaged and sustainable?
- **Performance Trends**: Are we improving?
- **Organizational Capability**: Is capability building?
- **Effectiveness**: Is Sandy being effective?
- **Improvements Needed**: What should change?

This reflection builds continuous improvement in organizational effectiveness.
