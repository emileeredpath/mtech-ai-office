## [2.0.0] - 2026-07-17

### Added - SYSTEM 01: Sandy (Operational Brain)

SYSTEM 01 - Sandy is the complete operational system for AI Office V2. Sandy is the Chief Operating Officer, Chief of Staff, and operational brain that coordinates all organizational work, preserves institutional knowledge, maintains quality standards, and ensures strategic alignment.

#### Core Documents (Foundational)
- **01.00 Who is Sandy**: Identity, role, organizational context, operating mindset
- **01.01 Purpose**: Strategic contribution, purpose statement, five primary purposes
- **01.02 Responsibilities**: Nine core responsibility areas with specific accountability
- **01.03 Authority**: Decision-making authority across four tiers; escalation requirements
- **01.21 Rules Sandy Never Breaks**: Ten non-negotiable principles (Critical Reading)

#### Operational Framework (How Sandy Works)
- **01.04 Daily Operations**: Five-phase daily rhythm (planning, receiving, delegating, monitoring, closing)
- **01.05 Delegation Framework**: Six-step delegation process; specialist coordination
- **01.06 Decision Making**: Decision frameworks for different decision types
- **01.07 Prioritisation Framework**: 4x4 prioritization matrix; handling conflicting priorities
- **01.08 Project Management**: Five-phase project lifecycle; project tracking
- **01.09 Employee Management**: Specialist coordination; development; challenging situations

#### Support Systems (Organizational Visibility)
- **01.10 Knowledge Management**: Six types of organizational memory; capture and preservation
- **01.11 Dashboard Management**: Six core dashboards; designing dashboards
- **01.12 Meetings**: Meeting philosophy; regular meetings schedule; meeting structure
- **01.13 Reporting**: Report types; content principles; distribution; escalation reporting

#### Strategic and Improvement (Organizational Excellence)
- **01.14 Continuous Improvement**: Identifying improvements; prioritizing; implementing; building culture
- **01.15 Crisis Management**: Crisis recognition; five-phase response; team roles; communication
- **01.16 Communication Standards**: Eight principles; channels; standards; difficult conversations
- **01.17 Reviewing Work**: Work review timing; review process; feedback conversations
- **01.18 Memory Management**: Capturing memory; organization; currency; learning cycles
- **01.19 Automation Oversight**: Identifying automation; planning; implementation; monitoring

#### Performance and Guidance (Measurement and Non-Negotiables)
- **01.20 Success Metrics**: Eight core metrics; leading vs. lagging indicators; using metrics
- **01.21 Rules Sandy Never Breaks**: Ten non-negotiable rules; what they enable; handling conflicts

#### Routines (Time Management)
- **01.22 End of Day Routine**: Seven-step process; checklist; closing out work
- **01.23 Weekly Routine**: Weekly meeting schedule; four hours of analysis; reflection
- **01.24 Monthly Routine**: Monthly meetings; six to eight hours of analysis; reporting

#### Strategic Evolution
- **01.25 Future Vision**: Evolution stages; role evolution; technology evolution; long-term success

#### System Documentation
- **README.md**: Complete guide to SYSTEM 01; how to use; core principles; governance

SYSTEM 01 - Sandy represents a complete operational system for AI Office V2. It is:

- **Comprehensive**: Covers all aspects of organizational operations
- **Detailed**: Specific enough to guide action; not abstract

6. **Strategic Alignment**: Daily work connects to objectives
7. **Continuous Improvement**: Organization becomes better over time
